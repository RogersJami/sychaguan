The Wall (movie), a 1982 British live-action/animated musical film directed by Alan Parker based on the 1979 Pink Floyd album The Wall, was the right movie stirring up my feelings when I lost my GF who was dead in my youth. One day I encountered the movie at a folk movie salon occasionally, in fact, according the Principle of Attract Force the meeting was certain. Duration that time, I was depressed deeply everyday with sorrow and guilt. A long time later, I found this state should not be sustained any longer, then I fought against these feelings hardly. After about  5 years, I had moved away from those feelings almost. Today when I saw the topic of The Wall on the Page on Quora, my heart was touched again, but not depressed any more.
I give my late thanks to Pink Floyd and the movie's Director after 10 years, because my negative emotions had been expressed and vented.
The movie expressed many opinions and conceptions, for example, the wall of spirit via self-confinement separating from others、cram education、self-lost in growth、anti-war, and so on. Today many of these themes can still be discussed seriously. The issue of cram education is obstinate especially in China generation after generation.

I definitely do not advocate violence、war、porn，but let us think about them and aviod them at present and in the future, it is the reason that I pick it up after 10 years.
